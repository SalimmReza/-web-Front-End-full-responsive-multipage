Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics

Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, colloquially known as Nanhang, is a public research university in Nanjing, China.
It is part of the Project 211 and a Chinese Ministry of Education Double First Class Discipline University, with First Class status in certain disciplines.
